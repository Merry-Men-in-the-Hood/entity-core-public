# $ENTITY Token Launch Criteria

The $ENTITY token launches **only when conditions are met**.
There is no fixed launch date. The token is not the goal - defense is
the goal. The token is one bootstrap mechanism among several.

## Required Conditions (ALL must be met)

- [ ] **System stable** - the aggregator has run reliably without major incidents
- [ ] **Track record** - Entity has published threat warnings with demonstrated value
- [ ] **Real demand** - the public API serves sustained organic usage from real users, showing genuine demand for Entity's intelligence
- [ ] **Community ready** - an organic community has formed, with no dominant negative narrative
- [ ] **Founder readiness** - not exhausted, not rushed, prepared for the responsibility a launch carries

## Token Mechanics (when launched)

- **Platform:** a fair-launch on Base if a launch is ever warranted; specific venue undecided
- **Allocation:** 100% public - no team allocation, no pre-sale, no insider rounds
- **Fees:** 50-70% of trading fees flow to Entity treasury
- **Utility:** governance only - no profit-share, no security token

## Why Defer

A token launched too early without:
- Working product ⇒ rug-pull pattern, retail loses money
- Community ⇒ no organic demand, dies fast
- Track record ⇒ no narrative anchor, becomes purely speculative

A token launched at the right moment:
- Captures audience that has seen Entity perform
- Funds long-term operational autonomy
- Aligns community with Entity's mission via governance

## What Happens Until Launch

Entity operates on:
- Donations
- Grant funding (apply parallel)
- Agent-hired tasks via x402 endpoints (deferred)
- Custom contracts (Phase 2+)
- Founder runway buffer (bootstrap window only)

If conditions are never met within 12 months, launch is reconsidered or
permanently deferred. Entity does not need a token to exist.

## Decision Authority

Token launch decision requires **2-of-3 founder multisig + 7-day
public comment period** before deployment.

If 2-of-3 founders reach launch consensus, a public announcement is made
with 7 days notice. During that window, community feedback is gathered.
If concerns surface, launch is delayed.

## What Token Holders Get

- Governance voting on Entity treasury allocations beyond operational
  spending
- Voice in Entity's strategic direction (Layer 1/2/3 priorities)
- Ability to propose new endpoint offerings, partnerships
- **No right to operational override** - Immutable Core remains
  immutable; Prime Directives cannot be voted away

## What Token Holders Do NOT Get

- Profit share from Entity revenue
- Right to halt Entity operations
- Authority over Immutable Core
- Authority over Operational Prompt
- Discretionary access to treasury beyond governance proposals
- Identification of pseudonymous founders

## When This Document Updates

Any change to launch criteria requires same process as launch:
2-of-3 founder multisig + 7-day public comment.

Last updated: launch day.
